Diploma in Computer Science

This program introduces students to programming fundamentals, data structures, algorithms, databases, web development, and networking. Students will also explore emerging technologies such as AI and cloud computing.

Key Subjects:

  • Introduction to Programming (Python/C++)
  • Web Development (HTML, CSS, JS)
  • Database Systems (SQL, MongoDB)
  • Networking Fundamentals
  • Software Engineering
  • Operating Systems

Duration: 2.5 years
Entry Requirements: SPM or equivalent with a minimum of 3 credits including Math.
