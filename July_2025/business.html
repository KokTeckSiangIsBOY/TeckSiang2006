Diploma in Business Administration

Our Business Administration diploma equips students with a strong foundation in the world of commerce, preparing them for dynamic careers in both public and private sectors. Through real-world case studies and projects, students develop strategic thinking and leadership skills.

Key Subjects:

  • Principles of Management
  • Marketing & Consumer Behavior
  • Business Law
  • Accounting & Finance
  • Entrepreneurship
  • Human Resource Management

Program Details:

Duration: 2.5 years
Entry Requirements: SPM or equivalent with minimum 3 credits including English or Mathematics
