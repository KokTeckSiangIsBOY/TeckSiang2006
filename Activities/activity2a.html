In HTML, paragraphs are defined with the p tag.
Aparagraph element in an HTML document is made up of content
such as text, images, or other content like form fields which appens between opening p tag and closing p tag.

p tag is the HTML tag for a paragraph. Adding the p tag to an HTM doc,
folloed by text, will create a paragraph on your page.
