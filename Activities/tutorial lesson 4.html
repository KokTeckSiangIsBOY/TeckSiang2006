Short Story:The Shepherd boy and the Wolf

A shepherd boy in village used to take his herd of sheep across the fields near the forest. He felt this job was very dull and wanted to have some fun.

One day while grazing the sheep, he shouted, "wolf! Wolf! The Wolf is carrying away a lamb!" Farmers working in the nearby fields came runnign for help but didn't find any wolf.

The boy laughed and replied,"It was just fun.There is no wolf here". The boy played a similar trick repeatly for many days.

After some days, while the shepherd boy was in the field with the herd of sheep, suddenly, suddenly, a wolf came out of nearby forest and attacked one of the lambs. The boy was frightened and cried loudly. "Wolf! Wolf!The wolf is carrying a lamb away!"

The Farmersshoughtthe boy was playingmischiefagain.So no one paid attention to him and didn't come to his help.

Moral: No one believe a liar even if they speak the truth once.
